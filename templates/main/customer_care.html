You can use our Risk Estimator tool which evaluates your crop details, location, and other factors to recommend suitable insurance plans. Alternatively, you can contact our support team for personalized guidance.

You can file a claim through your dashboard by going to "My Insurances" and selecting the relevant policy. You'll need to provide photos of the damaged crops and fill out a claim form. An agent will be assigned to verify your claim.

After you file a claim, an agent will typically verify it within 3-5 business days. Once verified, the claim amount is usually disbursed within 7-10 business days, depending on your bank and the payment method.
