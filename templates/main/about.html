About AgriSure

Our Vision

To create a risk-free agricultural ecosystem where every farmer can focus on growing crops without worrying about financial losses due to calamities.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and transparent crop insurance solutions to all farmers across India, leveraging technology to simplify the process and ensuring fair, quick claim settlements.

Our Story

AgriSure was founded in 2022 by a team of agricultural experts and technology enthusiasts who witnessed firsthand the struggles of farmers when faced with crop failures. Despite the existence of insurance schemes, many farmers found the processes complicated, the settlements delayed, and coverage inadequate.

We set out to change this narrative by building a platform that puts farmers first. By leveraging technology, we've simplified the entire insurance journey—from selection to claims—making it accessible even in remote rural areas through mobile devices.

Today, AgriSure works with multiple insurance providers, government schemes, and agricultural organizations to offer comprehensive coverage options tailored to different crops, regions, and farming practices.

Our Values

Trust & Transparency

We believe in clear communication and transparent processes that build lasting trust with farmers.

Speed & Efficiency

Quick responses and efficient processes that respect the time-sensitive nature of farming.

Farmer-First Approach

Every decision we make prioritizes the welfare and convenience of farmers.

Sustainability

Supporting sustainable farming practices for long-term agricultural resilience.
